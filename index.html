About

I am a motivated student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Actuarial Science, with a growing focus on risk management, finance, and analytics. My academic journey is centred on developing the technical knowledge, analytical discipline, and quantitative mindset needed to support sound financial planning and effective risk-based decision-making.

I am also deeply interested in the wider business environment, where finance, data, and strategy shape real outcomes. With curiosity, commitment, and a strong desire to keep learning, I am building myself into a professional who can contribute meaningfully to financial stability, informed analysis, and responsible decision-making.
